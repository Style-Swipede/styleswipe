2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser on subsequent visits, store preferences, and statistically analyze usage of our website.

3. Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Technically necessary cookies: Required for the website’s functionality and cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Enable additional features such as language selection or user settings.
  • Analytics and statistics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.
  • Marketing and tracking cookies: Used to display targeted content or advertisements. We only use these with your consent.

4. Legal Basis

We use technically necessary cookies based on Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest).

Other cookies are only used with your consent in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR.

5. Changing Cookie Settings

You can withdraw or adjust your consent at any time via our cookie banner. Alternatively, you can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.

6. Storage Duration

The duration of cookie storage varies. Session cookies are deleted after the browser is closed. Other cookies remain stored until deleted or automatically expire.

7. Third-Party Providers & Tools

If we use third-party analytics or advertising tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Awin), cookies are also set by those providers. Details can be found in our Privacy Policy.
