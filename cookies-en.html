2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser, remember settings, and analyze site usage.

3. Types of Cookies

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Essential for basic site functionality.
  • Functional cookies: Remember settings like language and preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Provide insights into website usage.
  • Marketing cookies: Help deliver personalized advertising. Only used with your consent.

4. Legal Basis

Strictly necessary cookies are based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest). All other cookies require your consent under Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

5. Changing Cookie Settings

You can update your preferences via the cookie banner or block/delete cookies in your browser settings.

6. Storage Duration

Session cookies are deleted after your visit. Persistent cookies remain until manually deleted or expiration.
