2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser on future visits, save preferences, and analyze website usage.

3. Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the operation of the website and cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Enable features like language selection or user preferences.
  • Analytics and statistics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.
  • Marketing and tracking cookies: Used for personalized content or advertising, set only with your consent.

4. Legal Basis

Strictly necessary cookies are based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

All other cookies are used only with your consent according to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

5. Changing Cookie Settings

You can withdraw or adjust your consent at any time via our cookie banner. Alternatively, you can block or delete cookies through your browser settings.

6. Storage Duration

The duration varies. Session cookies are deleted when the browser is closed. Others remain until deleted or they expire.
