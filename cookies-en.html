2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser on subsequent visits, save preferences, and analyze usage of our website statistically.

3. Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the website’s functionality and cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Enable additional functions like language selection or user settings.
  • Analytics and statistics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.
  • Marketing and tracking cookies: Used to display targeted content or advertising. These are used only with your consent.

4. Legal Basis

Strictly necessary cookies are used based on Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest).

Other cookies are only used with your consent under Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR.

5. Changing Cookie Settings

You can revoke or adjust your consent anytime via our cookie banner. Alternatively, you can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.

6. Storage Duration

The storage duration of cookies varies. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Other cookies remain stored until they expire or are deleted.

7. Third Parties & Tools
